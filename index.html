About Me

I am an associate professor in College of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics. I obtained my PhD from Southeast University in 2024, where I was very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xin Geng.

Research Interests

My research interests include topics in machine learning and artificial intelligence. Most recently, I am interested in

  • Multimodal Learning, Multimodal LLMs

  • Multi-Label Learning, Label Distribution Learning

  • Reinforcement Learning, Embodied Intelligence
